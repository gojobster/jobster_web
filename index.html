How It Works

Each month, more than 7 million Jobhunt turn to website in their search for work, making over
160,000 applications every day.

Become a Jobster

Once you sign up and configure your jobster profile, you can start referring people from your network to the open job offers. The candidates you refer will receive an email with the information about the job you recommend them for. If they like the offer, we will add them to the candidate list for that position.

The algorithm kicks in

Once the job offer expires, we compile all the profiles and rank them based on the candidates' fitness and other parameters. After the filtering is done, we give the job poster a short list of 3 to 5 candidates..

They get a job, you get a reward

If a candidate you referred gets hired, you get a bonus of up to 1000€.
